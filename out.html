Salvaging
Category System

Salvaging is a System that allows you to exchange certain items for Essence or various materials.

Mechanics

Most items that can be salvaged only grant a specific type of Essence. For example, most Armor pieces dropped from Catacombs Mobs can be salvaged for Undead Essence.

If the item has a recipe, then you'll instead gain its ingredients, with the amounts given being each amount from the recipe divided by 2, rounded down. I t e m A m o u n t = f l o o r ( R e c i p e A m o u n t 2 ) {\displaystyle \color {BurntOrange}ItemAmount=floor({\frac {RecipeAmount}{2}})}

If the item has ✪ stars, then each ✪ star on the item grants a bonus +2 Essence of the same type. Additionally, Dungeon Items with a +50% base Stat boost grant an additional +10 Essence of the same type.

Items that are Co-op Soulbound from being donated to the Museum cannot be salvaged.

Salvage Menu

You can insert up to 14 items into the Salvage Menu at once. There are options to insert all non-modified / non-upgraded UNCOMMON, RARE, or EPIC items from your inventory into the menu.
